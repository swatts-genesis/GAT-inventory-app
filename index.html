About

A simple offline inventory logger. Photos are stored locally on your device. Export your inventory as a ZIP file (CSV spreadsheet + photos folder) anytime — open the CSV in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets.

Storage

Inventory and photos are saved to this browser only. Clearing browser data will remove them — export regularly as backup.
